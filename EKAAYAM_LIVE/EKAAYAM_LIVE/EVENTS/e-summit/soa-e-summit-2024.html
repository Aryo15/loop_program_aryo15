The Startup Expo is a showcase event where startups at various stages can display their products, services, and technologies to attendees, including potential investors, customers, and partners. It's a great opportunity for networking and gaining visibility.

The Ideathon, on the other hand, is a competition where participants pitch innovative ideas to solve real-world problems to a panel of judges for a chance to win cash prizes and potential investment. It focuses more on the idea stage and conceptualization rather than an existing product or service.

The Ideathon is centered around conceptualizing and pitching an innovative idea. Participants are judged based on the novelty, feasibility, and potential impact of their ideas. It’s more about the initial stages of entrepreneurship and innovation.

The Hackathon (Idea-Prototype Hackathon) is a more hands-on event where teams develop a working prototype, either software or hardware, based on a given theme or problem statement within a limited time. It emphasizes technical skill, implementation, and the ability to create a tangible product or solution.

The ESummit events, including the keynote speeches, workshops, and competitions, will take place at the ITER Campus Auditorium.
